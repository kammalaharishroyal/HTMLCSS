Ayodhya Ram Mandir Inauguration Live Updates: The temple town of Ayodhya witnessed a historic moment on Monday today as the new Ram Lalla idol was consecrated at the newly constructed temple, amidst grand celebrations and religious fervor. Prime Minister Narendra Modi played a pivotal role in the rituals, marking a significant day in the cultural and spiritual history of India.

Upon his arrival, the Prime Minister entered the temple premises carrying a silver 'chattar' (umbrella) atop a red folded dupatta, symbolizing reverence and honor. Dressed in a traditional golden kurta, cream dhoti, and patka, PM Modi took a 'sankalp' or vow for the “pran pratishtha ceremony,” a ritual that breathes life into the deity's idol, before proceeding to the sanctum sanctorum to partake in the consecration rituals.

The ceremony was graced by the presence of several dignitaries, including RSS Chief Mohan Bhagwat, Uttar Pradesh Governor Anandiben Patel, and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. These leaders were present inside the sanctum sanctorum, participating in the historic and religiously significant event.

In a spectacular display of devotion and celebration, army helicopters adorned the sky, showering flower petals over the temple premises during the consecration. This act not only added to the grandeur of the occasion but also symbolized the nation's collective reverence and joy for this momentous event.

The consecration of the new Ram Lalla idol in the majestic temple signifies more than just a religious ceremony; it represents the culmination of years of anticipation and devotion for millions of believers. The event has drawn attention from across the nation, uniting people in a common thread of cultural and spiritual celebration. The grandeur of the celebrations in Ayodhya and the involvement of top national leaders underscore the profound importance of this event in the tapestry of India's rich heritage.
